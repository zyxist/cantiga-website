Membership management

Cantiga is a membership management and collaboration system targeting non-profit organizations and large, franchise-like social campaigns.

Built on practice

The project was originally designed and it is still used by one of the biggest non-profit events in Poland, Extreme Way of the Cross, with more than 100 local editions and 25000+ participants.

Open-source

You can use it and modify to meet your needs for free, because Cantiga is free and open-source software. And it always will.

it will work for you

because it's worked for us. This is why we open-sourced it.
