About Cantiga Project

... and why we do it

Cantiga is a membership management and collaboration system. It helps your non-profit organization managing your projects, and the volunteers - collaborating on them. It works for you, if your project is small, limited to an area of a single town or city, where everyone knows one another personally. Now they have a platform to collaborate remotely and track the project progress. It also works for you, as your project grows and expands into new areas. You get a tool, where local leaders from different cities can work together, and you always know what is going on.

Mission

Our mission is to help non-profit organizations run their projects. We do this by providing a high-quality, open-source platform that enables the organizations manage, collaborate and communicate seamlessly on their projects. We want our platform to scale as the projects grow, by implementing the best engineering and collaboration practices.

Case studies

Extreme Way of the Cross is a project that started in 2009 in Kraków. Over years, it attracted hundreds of thousands of people in Poland, Great Britain, Norway, Switzerland, USA, and other countries, to walk 40 kilometers at night through forests, bogs and mountains to practice the old, traditional Way of the Cross devotion. It even reached as remote areas, as Svalbard and Antarctica. The project is a joint work of hundreds of local leaders who run their own local editions in cooperation with central team which provides the know-how and the standards how the event should look like.

Extreme Way of the Cross uses Cantiga for the registration of new areas, and managing them. Area leaders can use the provided tools to publish their routes on the project website, and run the registration of participants. Cantiga is also a tool for tracking the project progress and training new leaders through the on-line course module.
